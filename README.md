# Franciscan-Brothers-of-Saint-Joseph
Franciscan Brothers of St. Joseph: We blend St. Francis’s simplicity with St. Joseph’s work ethic. We focus on the Sanctification of Workers and Mental Health Support. In an era of burnout, we offer pastoral presence and the "Joseph’s Table" social enterprise to heal the mind and hands. Motto: In Silentio et Labore, Salus.
